# Glossary  
Real Estate Decision Intelligence Platform

This document defines common terminology used across the system architecture, product design, and development process.

The glossary ensures consistent understanding between business stakeholders, advisors, and developers.

---

## Client

A registered user of the platform who is evaluating or investing in real estate assets.

Clients interact with the system to:

- build an investment profile
- explore recommended projects
- analyze opportunities
- track their purchase lifecycle

---

## Advisor

A professional who assists clients in evaluating and selecting real estate investments.

Advisors use the platform to:

- review client profiles
- recommend projects
- track decision progress
- manage client lifecycle

---

## Client Intelligence

The system module responsible for building and managing client investment profiles.

This includes:

- financial capacity
- risk tolerance
- investment horizon
- lifestyle preferences
- priority weighting

Client Intelligence enables the system to recommend projects based on real needs instead of generic listings.

---

## Project Intelligence

The structured dataset describing each real estate project.

This includes:

- project overview
- location intelligence
- legal status
- market indicators
- risk analysis
- investment thesis

Project Intelligence transforms raw project data into structured decision inputs.

---

## Matching Engine

The system responsible for calculating the compatibility between a client profile and a project.

The engine generates a **Project Fit Score** using multiple dimensions such as:

- financial match
- lifestyle compatibility
- investment potential
- risk alignment
- location suitability

---

## Project Fit Score

A numerical score representing how well a project matches a specific client profile.

The score ranges from:

0 → 100

Higher scores indicate stronger alignment between project characteristics and client priorities.

---

## Opportunity Board

The main discovery interface where clients explore recommended projects.

Projects are displayed as advisory cards highlighting:

- fit score
- investment grade
- key strengths
- potential risks

The Opportunity Board is designed for **curated decision support**, not for generic listing browsing.

---

## Decision Workspace

A dedicated interface where clients analyze and compare selected projects before making a purchase decision.

The workspace may include:

- project comparison
- advisor notes
- financial analysis
- decision checklist

---

## Lifecycle Management

The system responsible for tracking the progress of a client's real estate purchase.

Typical lifecycle stages include:

- exploring
- site visit
- reservation
- deposit
- SPA signing
- payment milestones

---

## Investment Grade

A classification assigned to a project based on investment metrics.

Example grading:

A — Strong investment potential  
B — Moderate investment potential  
C — Speculative opportunity  
D — High risk investment

---

## Decision Intelligence Platform

A system designed to assist users in making informed investment decisions.

Unlike traditional property listing platforms, this system focuses on:

- analysis
- compatibility scoring
- advisor insights
- lifecycle management
